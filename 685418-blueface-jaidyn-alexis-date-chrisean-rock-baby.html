When it comes to baby mama drama, no one has more on his plate lately than Blueface. The 26-year-old already shares two children with high school sweetheart Jaidyn Alexis, and he currently has one on the way with Crazy In Love co-star Chrisean Rock. The Baltimore-born entertainer announced news of her pregnancy back in January, and the months since have seen her and Blueface feuding near-constantly on social media. For the most part, it's been the Californian antagonizing her with his antics, but earlier this week, a video of her making plans with another man began making rounds on social media.

Since then, Blueface has been loudly and proudly showering his first baby mama with attention, seemingly attempting to make Chrisean jealous. At the same time, her Instagram feed has been lighting up with maternity content, from a video of her crying while praying in her kitchen to content from a stunning photoshoot organized by Blac Chyna for the first-time mother.

Blueface Spoils His First Baby Mama

For his part, Blueface took Jaidyn out on a date to begin his weekend, capturing plenty of their time together on his Story. "I'm not gonna watch the child so you gon' be stuck watching the child," he told her. "You think I'm gonna watch y'all's child?" Alexis immediately responded, the disinterest in her voice obvious. "Yeah," Blueface replied, "if we together." This earned laughter from his on-again-off-again lover, who clearly has a different vision than her man.
