Aubrey Plaza Wears Sleek White Dress with Cutouts to 2023 Met Gala

August 11, 2024
post

Aubrey Plaza's style streak has made it to the Met Gala red carpet.

The White Lotus star made an entrance at the event, held at the Metropolitan Museum of Art, tonight in a chic white gown with giant chest and waist cutouts by Jennifer Behr. The cutouts were embellished with thick silver bejeweled chains. The actress paired the unique floor-length gown with black leather opera gloves and diamond jewelry by EFFY Jewelry, including a bracelet, dramatic earrings, and a large ring. She had her hair up in a romantic updo, tied together with a black bow, and wore inky black eye liner and a glossy pink lip.

Dimitrios Kambouris//Getty Images

Plaza has been showing out on the red carpet, as of late. Just last week, she attended the Time100 Gala in red-hot Carolina Herrera minidress that was sexy, fun, and sophisticated all at once. The silky mini, which featured a cape overlay, hailed from the fashion house's fall/winter 2023 collection. She styled the piece with black pumps and Yvonne Léon jewelry: gold-and-diamond rings and black hoop earrings with small diamonds. As for glam, the actress had her hair in an elegant updo and wore inky-black eyeliner and a subtle pink lip.

And in February, Plaza attended the 2023 SAG Awards in a bronze sequined halter-neck gown from Michael Kors Collection with a large diamond-shaped midriff and underboob-baring cutout.
