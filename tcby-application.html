TCBY Application Online: Jobs &amp; Career Info

August 05, 2024
post

Are you planning to apply for a job at TCBY?

This company proudly calls itself “The Country’s Best Yogurt” and is the world’s largest frozen yogurt brand. TCBY was established in 1981 and operates more than 350 locations across the United States alone.

TCBY is constantly expanding, meaning that job vacancies of all types are advertised throughout the year. However, the popularity of the company means that competition for available positions can be rather high.

So, let’s take an in-depth look at the TCBY Application Online: Jobs & Career Info and find out how to appeal to their hiring managers.

Facts About Working At TCBY

You need to be at least eighteen years old to work at TCBY. The company’s hours of operation are 10:00 am to 9:00 pm from Monday to Saturday, and 11:00 am to 6:00 pm on Sundays. Employees are often required to work on weekends and evenings as well as during public holidays.

TCBY boasts outlets in more than thirty countries, including parts of the Middle East and Eastern Asia. People who want to discover a new country while earning a salary have the option of transferring to a different location. There are also plenty of opportunities for employees to advance within the company.

TCBY Employment Opportunities

TCBY offers a wide range of entry-level positions to new employees who have little or no work experience. Entry-level employees who excel are often offered promotions to leadership positions within the company. Let’s take a closer look at some of the main roles at TCBY, as well as the key duties that go with them.

Team Member

This entry-level position can involve a wide range of duties, such as preparing frozen yogurt and serving customers. Team members need to be on hand to greet customers and take their orders. They need to be familiar with the full TCBY product range and be able to make recommendations based on customers’ preferences.

Team members also need to be able to operate the cash register, provide the correct change, and keep the store clean. While no work experience is necessary, basic math and customer service skills are essential. Team members usually receive $9 per hour, and both full-time and part-time hours are available.

Assistant Manager

Entry-level employees who have been with the company for a few years are actively encouraged to take on this role. While assistant managers start at $9 per hour, they receive extensive paid training and pay raises. This is an excellent opportunity to gain new skills that can be used to advance further within the company.

Assistant managers are responsible for overseeing the daily operations within a single TCBY store. They need to monitor sales and ensure that transactions go smoothly. They need to be comfortable with delegating duties and ensuring that the store is clean at all times.

Manager

TCBY managers are usually in charge of a single store and are responsible for making sure it runs smoothly. They need to interview, hire, and train new employees and make sure they work at the optimum level. Managers also need to answer queries and complaints from customers and make sure they are satisfied.

This role also involves performing stock checks and ordering new stock when necessary. Managers need to be comfortable leading staff meetings, creating special promotions, and delegating tasks. TCBY managers usually earn around $33,000 per year, while those who excel are also treated to performance bonuses.

Additional Benefits of Working at TCBY

All TCBY employees are treated to discounts on frozen yogurt and other company products. Full-time managers and other professionals are also treated to an impressive benefits package, including access to special bonus programs and raises. Other benefits include dental, vision plans, and retirement plans, and general medical coverage.

How To Apply?

The TCBY application online can be found in the careers section of the company’s website. You first need to select your desired location from the dropdown menu and click on the job title you are interested in. Read through the job description carefully and adapt your resume so that it is ready to attach to your application.

The TCBY application online consists of four pages and is easy to fill out. You need to start by filling out personal information in the spaces provided. The next section is titled employment desired and requires you to fill out the job title and your available shift patterns.

References are needed…

There are spaces for your high school and college information, followed by three spaces for previous employment. You also need to supply the name and phone number of three different references. The last section consists of an applicant certification and agreement, which should be carefully read and then signed.

Final Thoughts

TCBY hiring managers actively seek candidates who are friendly and have good customer service skills. Employees are expected to be polite to customers at all times and be able to diffuse difficult situations.

Therefore, it is important to focus on your body language during the interview and remain friendly yet professional at all times.

The interview process for entry-level positions is generally fairly short and focuses on behavioral questions. Professional-level interviews such as interviews for managerial roles tend to be a bit longer and more in-depth. In both cases, it is a good idea to do background research into the company before the interview and prepare questions.
