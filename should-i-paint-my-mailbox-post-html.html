How do you paint a mailbox post?

To paint the mailbox post, remove the mailbox and any address numbers. Sand the post to eliminate any moss, mould or mildew. Then, fix any cracks or holes with wood filler or paste. Apply two layers of primer and after dry, apply the first coat of paint.

How do you paint an aluminium mailbox?

Apply a spray rust sealant to any rusty parts of the metal mailbox. Allow the sealer to cure for two hours, and then apply a layer of etching primer to the whole mailbox. Allow the etching primer to set for four hours, and then apply two coats of acrylic spray paint on the mailbox.
