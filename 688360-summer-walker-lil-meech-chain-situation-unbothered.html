Summer Walker &amp; Lil Meech: R&amp;B Singer Seems Unbothered By Chain Situation

August 27, 2024
post

Lil Meech is a talented actor, but that doesn't mean Summer Walker is looking for him to bring any drama into her life. Unfortunately for the mother of three, going public with the Florida native has done just that. Whether it's been Celina Powell sharing explicit videos of her and Meech to her OnlyFans, or more recently, another young woman posting a video wearing the BMF star's infamous chain on her Instagram Story.

We broke down all the drama earlier this week when Walker shared screenshots of her DMs with the girl. She claims that she never actually crossed paths with Meech, but rather, ran into his security and asked if she could wear the expensive jewelry. "My apologies if you feel disrespected, I can take the videos down if you'd like," Anisa Ali Abdu wrote back to the R&B diva before insisting that she's "not that type of woman." Afterward, the woman shared screenshots of her alleged messages with Lil Meech, revealing that he apparently offered to CashApp her money for making a post.

Summer Walker is Over the Drama

Despite all the messiness the world has seen online, it seems Walker is unbothered by the situation. Before kicking off her weekend, she's been sharing some sweet snapshots with Meech on her Story, revealing that they spent their night turning up together. "Not me getting drunk and talkin' 'bout Toys R'Us lmao. She's a muva," the "No Love" songstress wrote in a closed-eye selfie that shows off her beautiful jewelry.

In another selfie, she and her 23-year-old beau give the camera a serious stare. He keeps one hand on his face, subtly flaunting his luxury watch for all of his girlfriend's followers to see.
