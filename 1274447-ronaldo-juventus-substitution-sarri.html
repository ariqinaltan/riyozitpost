Cristiano Ronaldo storms into dressing room after being substituted against AC Milan

August 15, 2024

- Cristiano Ronaldo was substituted in Juventus 1-0 win over AC Milan ion a crunch Serie A match

- The Portuguese international went straight into the dressing room after coming off for Paulo Dybala

- Juventus won the match by 1-0 with 13 minutes left to play courtesy of a Dybala strike and move top of Serie A

Cristiano Ronaldo for the second time in as many days was not happy to be substituted in a Serie A contest against AC Milan.

The Portuguese international was taken off for Paulo Dybala after just nine minutes into the second half while the scores was still at 0-0.

The 34-year-old shook hands with his Argentine teammate and went straight into the dressing for an early shower.
