9 Alternatives To UGG Boots

August 26, 2024
post

Like most fashion trends from the early aughts, UGG boots have made their way back into our closets—and they’re here to stay (some may argue they never went away). A combo of slippers and boots, UGGs are certainly not the most flattering shoe—but you can’t deny how comfortable they are. And warm. What we don’t love about UGGs, though, is their high price tag. So naturally, we scoped out the best UGG boot alternatives out there. You can have your pick for under $100!

Our motto: Why pay more when you can get the same exact thing for half the price? No one will know the difference between these lookalikes and the real thing except for you—and your bank account. Whether you’re on the hunt for the iconic slip-on shearling boots or you’ve got your eyes on some of the more trendsetting UGG boot styles, we’ve discovered an UGG alternative for a range of the brand’s most popular footwear styles.

These UGG boot copycats are so spot on, we were nearly fooled when we looked at them—and we shop for a living. Ahead, shop these boots that’ll give UGG boots a run for their money. From boots with the fur to lookalikes of celeb-loved styles, there’s an UGG twin for every style and budget.

    If you love the bow detail of UGG Bailey Boots you can plunk down almost $260 for them — or you can grab a pair of Victoria Boots by Koolaburra by UGG, the brand’s lower-priced offshoot, for almost a third of the price. They’re doppelgangers in every way, including their molded EVA midsole and genuine sheepskin upper, though the fur is faux. “They fit perfectly. Warm and adorable,” wrote a wowed QVC customer.
