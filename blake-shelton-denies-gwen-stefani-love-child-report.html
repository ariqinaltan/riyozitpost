Gwen Stefani's new man Blake Shelton has laughed off rumours the pop star is pregnant. Photo/AP

Country star Blake Shelton has laughed off a new tabloid report suggesting Gwen Stefani is pregnant with his first child.

The Honey Bee singer went public with his budding new romance with Stefani last week, when The Voice co-stars were photographed holding hands as they headed to a party following the CMA Awards in Nashville, Tennessee.

Sources tell America's OK! magazine their relationship has become really serious and now Stefani is expecting his baby - but Shelton has made it clear the rumours are false.
