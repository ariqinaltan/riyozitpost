Bigg Boss Season 5 Malayalam Elimination 17 June 2023:- Bigg Boss Malayalam is one of the most popular reality shows in the Malayalam entertainment industry. The show is hosted by superstar Mohanlal and features a group of celebrities who live together in a house isolated from the outside world. The contestants have to face various tasks, challenges, and nominations every week, and one of them gets eliminated based on public voting.

The fifth season of Bigg Boss Malayalam started on 26 March 2023 and has been entertaining the viewers with its drama, comedy, and romance. The show has also witnessed some twists and turns, such as wild card entries, double eliminations, ticket-to-finale tasks, and secret rooms. As the show is nearing its grand finale, the competition among the contestants is getting tougher and the voting results are getting unpredictable.

Bigg Boss Malayalam 5 Nomination List – Week 16

The nomination process for the 16th week of Bigg Boss Malayalam 5 took place on Monday, 14 June 2023. The contestants had to nominate two of their fellow housemates by giving valid reasons. The contestants who received the maximum number of nominations were:

  • Akhil
  • Aniyan
  • Cerena
  • Junaiz
  • Nadira
  • Reneesha
  • Shiju

However, on Tuesday, 15 June 2023, Bigg Boss announced a twist in the nomination process. He gave a chance to the nominated contestants to save themselves from the danger zone by performing a task. The task was to collect as many coins as possible from a pool filled with water and mud. The contestant who collected the least number of coins would be directly eliminated from the show.

The task was performed by all the nominated contestants except Akhil, who was injured and could not participate. The task results were as follows:

  • Aniyan: 8 coins
  • Cerena: 7 coins
  • Junaiz: 6 coins
  • Nadira: 5 coins
  • Reneesha: 4 coins
  • Shiju: 3 coins

As per the task results, Shiju was directly eliminated from Bigg Boss Malayalam 5 on Tuesday night. He became the first contestant to be evicted in the 16th week.

Bigg Boss Malayalam 5 Elimination Prediction – Week 16

Based on the voting results so far, it seems that Akhil is leading the vote count by a huge margin and is safe from elimination this week. He is one of the strongest contestants in the show and has a huge fan base. He has also performed well in most of the tasks and has maintained good relations with most of the housemates.

On the other hand, Nadira and Reneesha are trailing behind in the vote count and are at a high risk of elimination this week. They are relatively new entrants in the show and have not been able to make a strong impact on the viewers or the housemates. They have also been involved in some arguments and controversies that may have affected their popularity.

Therefore, we predict that either Nadira or Reneesha will be eliminated from Bigg Boss Malayalam 5 this weekend.

However, this is just a prediction based on the current voting trends and nothing can change till the final announcement by Mohanlal on Sunday night. The viewers have the ultimate power to decide who stays and who leaves the show.

  • Step 1: Search for “Bigg Boss Vote” or “Bigg Boss Malayalam Vote” on Google.
  • Step 2: You will see the nominated contestants on the screen.
  • Step 3: Select your favorite contestant(s) to vote for.
  • Step 4: Click Submit to confirm your votes.

Note: You can vote a maximum of 10 votes a day.

How to Vote for Bigg Boss Malayalam 5 Online on Hotstar App?
