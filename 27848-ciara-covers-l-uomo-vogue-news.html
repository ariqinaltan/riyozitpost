Ciara Covers L'Uomo Vogue

August 12, 2024
post

Italian men's magazine L'Uomo Vogue had Nicki Minaj modeling for them last month, and in their new issue they get another American pop star to feature inside the magazine as well as the cover.

Ciara shared the cover on Instagram, and expressed her excitement to be the L'Uomo cover girl this month. She was photographed by the famed editor-in-chief Franca Sozzani, and the photo spread was strictly black & white.

While we may not be able to actually read the story, we can definitely enjoy the photographs. Take a look at them above.

Ciara hasn't released new music since 2013's self-titled album, understandably so as she's a new mom. Now that she's back in the press, we'll have to see if that means a new project in the works too.
