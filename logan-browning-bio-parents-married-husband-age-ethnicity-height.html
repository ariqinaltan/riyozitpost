Logan Browning is one of Hollywood’s rising stars. The actress has appeared in several films and TV sitcoms such as Bratz: The Movie, Brotherly Love, Meet the Browns, For the Better For the Worse, and Hit the Floor . She is most popular for the hit Netflix series, Dear White People , where she plays the lead role of Samantha White. Find out more about Browning here…

Age, early life and rise to fame

Records state that this actress was named LoganLaurice Browning after her birth on June 9, 1989 in Atlanta, Georgia. She spent her formative years in Georgia and attended Fayette Country High School. For her college education, Browning moved to Nashville, Tennessee, where she attended Vanderbilt University.

Logan Browning was an actress from a young age and attended Barbizon Modeling and Acting School by the age of 14. Summerland, where she played a character named Callie. From there she got into the Nickelodeon series, Ned’s declassified school survival guide and also appeared in a music video. Browning got her big break in 2007 when she scored a spot in Bratz: The Movie , a musical comedy. Although the film wasn’t a commercial success, she used it to land other roles.

Since then, the Atlanta native has appeared in other projects, most notably Tyler Perry’s Meet the Browns, Hit the Floor (a VH1 series), Kings Of Kings (a Disney series), The Secret Circle (a CW supernatural drama) and The Survivor’s Remorse. Browning’s most important project to date is undoubtedly the popular Netflix series Dear White People. In it she plays Samantha White, a college activist and radio host. The series began in 2017 and focuses on the issue of race relations in America. It received positive reviews from critics and fans, and Browning was acclaimed for her convincing portrayal of her character. In turn, she revealed that she had to do some heavy reading to prepare for the role.

Logan Browning Parents and Ethnicity

Logan Browning comes from a family of six. Among them are Larry, her father, his wife Lynda Browning, and three brothers – Keith, Clinton and Chad. Browning’s parents are both African American.

In May 2018, the actress revealed on TheBreakfast Club that she was in fact adopted and that her biological mother is white while her biological father is black. She had never met her and found out about her adoption at a young age. Due to the fact that her biological mother is white while her biological father is black, she is biracial – a mix of Caucasian and African American.

However, the actress identifies as black. In her words, she grew up in a black household and not a biracial one. She has emphasized that all of her experiences were those of a black person. When Browning was cast as the lead in Dear White People , she faced a backlash from some people who felt that the role should have gone to a person of darker complexion. She herself believes that her own life experiences make her the perfect match for Samantha, who has to navigate both sides of racial segregation.
