Five Guys National Cheeseburger Day: Does Five Guys Celebrate National Cheeseburger Day?

August 21, 2024
post

National Cheeseburger Day, an eagerly anticipated occasion among burger enthusiasts, takes place Monday September 18th this year and allows fast-food lovers to celebrate by indulging in some cheesy delights! With global giants like McDonald’s and Burger King already announcing their special deals, many wonder what other major players like Five Guys, Shake Shack, Wendy’s, and more have up their sleeves.

Five Guys: Keeping It Classic

While the excitement bubbles, it seems Five Guys has chosen to maintain its classic stance. As of the latest updates, the iconic American burger joint hasn’t unveiled any specific promotions or specials for National Cheeseburger Day this year. Neither their social media platforms nor their official website gives a hint about any celebratory offers.

Shake Shack: Midday Melt Delight

On the brighter side, Shake Shack has a tempting deal for the early birds. Only on September 18, the restaurant chain is rewarding its customers with a complimentary cheeseburger. The catch? Place your order through DoorDash between 11 a.m. and 2 p.m., punch in the code “SHACK”, and relish your free burger. Do keep an eye on additional charges like taxes and ensure you’re within the vicinity of the participating outlets.

Wendy’s: Stretching the Celebration

Wendy’s seems to be in no mood to limit the festivity to just one day. From September 18 to 22, they’re practically giving away their Jr. bacon cheeseburger for a mere $.01 with any purchase. To avail the offer, burger lovers can check the “Offers” tab on Wendy’s app or website. If visiting in person is more your style, simply get the deal added to your digital rewards card at the store.

Jack in the Box & Red Robin: Mellow on Offers

Just like Five Guys, Jack in the Box has chosen to stay on the sidelines, not introducing any special promotions for the day. Red Robin follows suit. A glance over their official channels, including Instagram, Twitter, and website, confirms the absence of National Cheeseburger Day-specific deals for 2023.

White Castle: Sliding into the Festive Mood

White Castle has their fans covered when it comes to celebrating special moments – making sure everyone feels included and included! From September 18 to 20, they’ve unveiled a ‘Buy One Get One’ offer on their Single Cheese Sliders, ensuring that the spirit of the day (and the subsequent two days) remains high.

A Day of Cheese and Choices

National Cheeseburger Day 2023 brings with it a mixed bag of offers. While some establishments have chosen to go all out, others have opted for a quieter approach. Regardless of where you choose to celebrate, one thing is certain: there’s no better day to appreciate the glorious combination of cheese, meat, and buns. Whether you’re benefiting from a sizzling deal or just enjoying your favorite burger, the essence of the day remains the same: to celebrate the humble cheeseburger and all the joy it brings.
