Is Jesse Watters still on The Five?

CONSERVATIVE political commentator Jesse Watters became a household name after he became a co-host of the roundtable series The Five.

On June 26, 2023, it was revealed that the Fox News political commentator would be taking over the network's evening primetime slot following Tucker Carlson's departure in April.

Is Jesse Watters still on The Five?

Jesse Watters is a co-host on The Five - which he joined back in April 2017 - as well as a host of Jesse Watters Primetime, which debuted in January 2022 on Fox News.

He also previously hosted the Saturday talk show, Watters' World but the show ended in January 2022.

On June 26, 2023, it was revealed that Watters would be taking over the network's 8pm hour, a role that was previously held by Tucker Carlson.

Greg Gutfeld, Laura Ingraham, Sean Hannity, and Trace Gallagher, will also occupy primetime blocks.

"Fox News Channel has been America’s destination for news and analysis for more than 21 years and we are thrilled to debut a new lineup,” Fox News Media CEO Suzanne Scott said in a statement.

“The unique perspectives of Laura Ingraham, Jesse Watters, Sean Hannity, and Greg Gutfeld will ensure our viewers have access to unrivaled coverage from our best-in-class team for years to come.”

The new lineup is expected to take effect on July 17, 2023.

Despite his new role, Watters will reportedly remain a co-host on The Five.

Is Jesse Watters married?

Watters was previously married to Noelle Inguagiato, a fashion stylist and television personality.

The two divorced in 2018 and share 10-year-old twin daughters, Ellie and Sophie.

Watters proposed to Emma DiGiovine, a journalist and producer, in August 2019.

The couple tied the knot in December of that year and share a son, Jesse Jr, and a daughter, Georgina.
