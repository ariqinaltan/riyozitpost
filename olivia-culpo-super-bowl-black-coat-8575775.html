See Olivia Culpo's Super Bowl Outfit

August 19, 2024
post

It may feel like every celebrity in Las Vegas is supporting the Kansas City Chiefs, but Olivia Culpo isn't one of them. After all, the model and actress is engaged to running back Christian McCaffrey, who plays for the San Francisco 49ers. So, she skipped out on the red 'fit and arrived at Allegiant Stadium in an all-black outfit that's very much in line with TikTok's mob wife vibe.

Arriving at the big game, Culpo wore a sleek, long black coat that looked like an old money staple, but when she turned around, her beau's name and number were affixed to the jacket, making for a sporty twist to an otherwise low-key outfit. Under the coat, she wore a black dress, and she added dramatic hoop earrings and a diamond-encrusted 23 necklace as a not-so-subtle nod to her fiancé.

Culpo and McCaffrey got engaged in April 2023. People noted that as soon as the two made their relationship public, Culpo has been a supportive girlfriend MVP. In fact, when McCaffrey got traded to the 49ers, she posted a message that may just have won the heart of every Niner faithful.

"I’m so proud of you, Christian! Seeing your light shine is one of my favorite things in life, and I’m beyond excited and grateful for this next chapter in Cali," she shared on Instagram. "Niners Nation, let’s go!"

And as soon as the team nabbed a spot in the Super Bowl, Culpo posted another message congratulating her fiancé. “Watching the love of your life live out his dream is the best feeling in the world,” she shared in a video that showed the couple hugging.

"You deserve it all, Christian ❤️,” she captioned the video. “I love you so so much.”
