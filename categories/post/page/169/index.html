Tony Stewart’s wife, Leah Pruett, is an American drag racer, currently driving an NHRA Top Fuel dragster for Tony Stewart Racing. She previously raced for Don Schumacher Racing and Bob Vandergriff Racing before they dissolved. She is a three-time National Event Pro Mod winner and an NHRA Nostalgia FC champion. Interestingly, her husband is also an American former professional car racing driver. Leah Pruett is a remarkable and accomplished figure in the world of professional motorsports.
