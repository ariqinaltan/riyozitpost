Congratulations are in order for a young man who recently gifted himself an expensive ride.

Abdul-Qawiyy took to Twitter to share the beautiful car he bought. The car which is a McLaren 720s, according to CarPrice, costs a whopping $311,130 (N118 million).

An excited Abdul-Qawiyy said he was proud of himself as he got to tick off an item that has long been on his bucket list.
