Rihanna Wore This Love-It-Or-Hate-It Boot Style to a Date Night With A$AP Rocky

August 12, 2024
post

It’s not every day that Rihanna steps out for date night with A$AP Rocky in New York City (especially since welcoming her second child, Riot), but when she does, she’s sure to add a fashionable twist to the city’s unofficial uniform of black-on-black.

On Tuesday, the singer was spotted exiting celeb-loved Carbone arm-in-arm with her beau after celebrating his 34th birthday in style. For the occasion, Rih slipped into a voluminous black tulle bustier corset top layered underneath a matching black cropped bomber jacket and paired with Balenciaga pantaboots, also in the color black. The mother-of-two accessorized with a simple diamond necklace and oversized silver hoop earrings, and she kept a pair of black rectangular sunglasses on (even at night) to further add to her cool girl vibe.

As for glam, Rihanna pulled her hair up into a messy ‘90s-style updo (save for her side-sweeping bangs) complete with a deep side part, and she rounded out the look by adding rosy cheeks, glowy contour, and a brown-lined pink lip.

For his part, A$AP wore a khaki check blazer paired with a gray crew neck sweater, baggy blue jeans, and black leather shoes. Peal-covered clips adorned the rapper’s hair, and he finished his ‘fit with a bubblegum pink Bottega Veneta bag.

The couple’s outing comes nearly two months after Rih secretly gave birth to her and Rocky’s second child, a son whose name was recently revealed to be Riot Rose Mayers, back in early August, reportedly making their family “complete.”

“Rihanna feels her family is now complete,” a source told People of baby Riot, who joins the couple’s 1-year-old son RZA at home. “It’s something she’s always wanted. She loves being a mom, so this is where her mind is at the moment.”

A source for Entertainment Tonight added, “Rihanna and A$AP are great right now. They’re so excited to grow their family and are so united when it comes to parenting. They have so much fun together and are very in love. They already shared a special connection, and parenting together has made their bond even stronger.”
