Matt Reigle - Grunge

August 21, 2024
post
  • Matt is a freelance writer with several years of experience writing for digital media.
  • In college, he started a blog called The Matt Reigle Files, which consists of humorous takes on sports, pop culture, and everyday things. He then worked as a local news reporter in rural Pennsylvania before doing some work for the website BroBible.
  • Matt has written two books. The first is a collection of essays and short stories called "Quarter-Life Manifesto: Rants, Ramblings, and Lies From an Idiot's First Twenty-Five Years Of Mediocrity," while the other is a comedy/science fiction novel called "They Came From Fort Blue Hill."

Experience

Matt is a writer living in Orlando, FL. He enjoyed telling stories even from an early age, and would even bug his elementary school teacher to let him give presentations to the class on unexplained phenomena like the Mothman or the Jersey Devil. He discovered his love of writing while working on the University of Central Florida's late-night comedy show "ToKnight's the Night," where he wrote comedy sketches and monologue jokes. After graduation, he returned to his hometown in Central Pennsylvania and worked for a local news website before writing sports and pop culture articles for the men's website BroBible. In his free time, Matt is a huge fan of the Philadelphia Flyers, enjoys watching any form of auto racing, and playing guitar.

Education

Matt graduated from the University of Central Florida in 2018 where he studied both Radio-Television and Creative Writing.
