What is Niecy Nash Net Worth and Salary?

Niecy Nash is an American actress, comedian, and television host known for her roles in Reno 911, Getting On, and Claws. As of 2021, her net worth is estimated to be $5 million. She has earned her fortune from her successful acting career, hosting jobs, and some endorsement deals.

Niecy Nash has also garnered good salaries for some of her most notable roles. According to sources, she earned $60,000 per episode for her role in the TNT drama Claws. Additionally, she has also hosted various shows like Clean House where she earned $30,000 per episode and has appeared on reality shows like The Masked Singer, where her salary is undisclosed.

Early Life and Education

Carol Denise Ensley, known professionally as Niecy Nash, was born on February 23, 1970, in Palmdale, California. Nash was raised by her mother, Margaret Ensley, and father, Sonny Nash, alongside four siblings.

She attended California State University, Dominguez Hills, where she took an interest in acting and later graduated with a degree in drama. Early in her career, Nash worked as a nurse’s assistant and a postal worker before venturing into the entertainment industry in the early 1990s.

Career

After a few minor roles on television shows like Boy Meets World and The Bernie Mac Show, Niecy Nash rose to prominence for her role as Deputy Raineesha Williams in Comedy Central’s Reno 911! in 2003. Her outstanding performance in the show earned her more television roles and positioned her as a noteworthy comedian.

In 2012, Niecy landed a significant role on the HBO comedy series, Getting On, playing Nurse Denise “DiDi” Ortley. Her portrayal of the character was critically acclaimed and earned her a Primetime Emmy Award nomination for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series.

She has also hosted various reality shows, including Clean House from 2003 to 2010 and the Style Network’s home-makeover show, Niecy Nash’s Home Court, in 2010. Nash has also appeared on Dancing with the Stars, The Insider, and The View.

In 2017, Niecy Nash joined the cast of the TNT drama series, Claws, and was nominated for a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Drama Series for her role as Desna Simms.

Personal Life

Niecy Nash was previously married to Rev. Don Nash, a producer, and musician. They were married in 1994, and the couple has three children together. However, they decided to end their marriage in 2007.

In 2011, Katy Perry asked Nash to play her in her music video for the song “California Gurls.” The process of becoming Perry required Nash to be drenched in cake frosting, which she overcame; her participation in the video drew her more attention from mainstream audiences.

Niecy Nash tied the knot with musician Jay Tucker in May 2011 at the Church Estate Vineyards in Malibu, California. But after eight years of marriage, the couple filed for divorce in December 2019.

Real Estate

In 2014, Niecy Nash sold her Bell Canyon, California home for $1.21 million after listing it for $1.149 million. The house which was built-in 1979, measures about 3,935 square feet.

In January 2021, she purchased her current 4,075 square feet Mediterranean-style home in Sherman Oaks, California, for $2.36 million.

Legacy

Niecy Nash has made an incredible impact on the entertainment industry. Her humor and ability to engage audiences quickly became her trademark appeal. Besides being recognized for her comedic roles on television, she has been an inspiration to many women, particularly those of color, who see her as a role model for success in the entertainment industry.
