How old is Demi Sims?

The youngest of the Sims sisters on the show, Demi is a Virgo born on September 6th 1996, making her 25 years old.

Making her first appearance on TOWIE in 2014, Demi has since landed far more screen time and become a well-known figure among the show’s fanbase.

Aside from her reality TV acting role, Demi is also a make-up artist and brand ambassador for clothing brands including Missy Empire and Pretty Little Thing.

Is Demi Sims in a relationship?

Coming out at the age of 19, Demi is bisexual and her most recent relationship was with Too Hot To Handle star, Francesca Farago.

The whirlwind romance lasted 4 months, in which time Francesca was introduced on-screen with Demi’s TOWIE cast members, however, the pair split in April 2021.

Before her relationship with Francesca, Demi had previous girlfriends who were also in the public eye. Following her appearance on Channel 4’s Celebs Go Dating, Demi hit it off with fellow cast member, Love Island’s Megan Barton-Hanson.

Despite their relationship ending shortly after the show, Demi then also entered a relationship with MTV’s Ex On The Beach star Leonie McSorley.

Demi now appears to remain single, with no photos of any significant other on her Instagram.

Demi Sims’ Instagram explored

An Instagram featuring many selfies and posts of her fashionable outfits, Demi boasts 600K followers.

Having most recently taken a trip to Ibiza, Demi’s Instagram is full of luxurious holiday posts to locations including Mexico, Turkey and Greece.

Despite not having a current partner, Demi introduced the newest member of her family to her followers in May 2021 with her adorable new miniature dachshund, Benji.
