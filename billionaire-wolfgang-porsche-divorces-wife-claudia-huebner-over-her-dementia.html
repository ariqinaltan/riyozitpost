Billionaire Wolfgang Porsche divorces wife Claudia Huebner over her dementia

August 30, 2024
post

Porsche executive Wolfgang Porsche has filed for divorce from his wife Claudia Huebner.

A source told German news site Bild that Claudia, 74, has a “dementia-like illness” that has changed her personality – which has made living together impossible.

Claudia – who formerly worked as an adviser to the German government – reportedly has been living near her daughter for about two years. She has four carers who look after her 24 hours a day.

She has not been able to move independently for months and her mental health has rapidly declined.

German news outlets have reported that since the separation, Wolfgang has been spending more time with his close friend of 25 years, 59-year-old Gabriela Prinzessin zu Leiningen.

Wolfgang was born in Austria and is the youngest son of Ferry Porsche, who founded the iconic sports car brand in Stuttgart in 1930.
