How old do you have to be to go to the Best Western?

August 16, 2024
post

Visiting a Best Western hotel can be an exciting experience, whether you are going for a family vacation, a business trip, or a leisurely getaway. If you are wondering about the age requirements for staying at a Best Western hotel, let’s explore the policies in place.

To stay at a Best Western hotel, the minimum age requirement is typically 18 years old. However, keep in mind that some locations may have different age restrictions. It’s always best to check with the specific hotel you plan to stay at, as they may have their own policies and guidelines. In addition, if you are under 21 years old, you may be required to provide a credit card for incidentals and a valid form of identification upon check-in.

FAQs about age requirements at Best Western hotels:

1. Is there a maximum age limit for staying at a Best Western hotel?
There is no maximum age limit for staying at a Best Western hotel. As long as you meet the minimum age requirement, which is typically 18 years old, you can book a room.

2. What if I am under 18 years old?
If you are under 18 years old, you may still be able to stay at a Best Western hotel. However, you will need to be accompanied by an adult who is responsible for you during your stay. This adult would need to provide consent and take full responsibility for your actions during your time at the hotel.

3. Can I book a room if I am under 21 years old?
Yes, you can book a room at a Best Western hotel if you are under 21 years old. However, some locations may have additional requirements, such as a higher security deposit or the need for a parent or guardian to co-sign the reservation.

4. Do I need a credit card to book a room?
Yes, most Best Western hotels require a credit card to reserve a room. This is used to secure your reservation and may also be used for incidentals. If you do not have a credit card, it’s recommended to contact the hotel directly to inquire about alternative payment options.

5. What forms of identification do I need to provide?
Upon check-in, you will typically be required to provide a valid form of identification. This can be a driver’s license, passport, or any government-issued ID. The purpose of this is to verify your identity and ensure the reservation matches the person checking in.

6. Can I check-in without an ID?
No, you cannot check-in without a valid form of identification. This is a standard procedure followed by hotels to ensure the safety and security of their guests.

7. Are there any age restrictions for hotel amenities?
Some Best Western hotels may have age restrictions for specific amenities, such as access to the pool, fitness center, or on-site bar. These restrictions are usually in place for the safety and well-being of all guests, especially if the amenities are not suitable for younger individuals.

8. Do I need to be a Best Western Rewards member to book a room?
No, you do not need to be a Best Western Rewards member to book a room. However, becoming a member can provide you with additional benefits, such as earning points for future stays and receiving exclusive offers.

9. Can I book a room for someone else who is under 18?
If you are over 18 years old, you can book a room for someone who is under 18. However, you would need to accompany them during their stay and take responsibility for their actions.

10. What if I have special requirements or accessibility needs?
Best Western hotels strive to accommodate guests with special requirements or accessibility needs. It is recommended to contact the hotel directly before making a reservation to discuss your specific needs and ensure they can provide suitable accommodations.

Remember, it’s always important to check with the specific Best Western hotel you plan to stay at for their exact age requirements and policies. While 18 years old is the general minimum age, individual hotels may have their own variations. By knowing the requirements in advance, you can ensure a smooth and enjoyable stay at the Best Western hotel of your choice.
