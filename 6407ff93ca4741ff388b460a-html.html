'Thanks for slapping Chris Rock': Michael Jackson's nephew praises Will Smith

August 25, 2024
post

While many are praising Chris Rock's latest Netflix stand-up special, the late Michael Jackson's family and fans are not on board.

In Chris Rock: Selective Outrage, the comedian compared the late King of Pop to convicted R&B singer R Kelly, joking they committed the "same crime", but only one was subject to outrage.

Jackson called Rock a bully for his Michael Jackson joke

Taj Jackson, Michael's nephew, took to Twitter to call Chris Rock a "bully" and thanked Will Smith for slapping him at the 2022 Oscars.

Chris Rock has used my family as punching bags for his entire career

Taj Jackson

"Chris Rock has used my family as punching bags for his entire career. Yet I am supposed to feel bad for him getting slapped and humiliated on the Oscars", Jackson wrote.

Tito Jackson's son Taj, original 3T group member, proceeded to make a list of three things he had to say to Chris Rock: "After seeing a new clip of him attacking my dead uncle in the first minutes of his 'Retaliation-I'm still relevant' special, I have 3 things to say."

Taj Jackson had a list of three things to say to Chris Rock

  • "What did my family ever do to you to warrant these decades of harassment and your constant bullying disguise as jokes?"
  • "Just because you were bullied early on in life doesn't give you the excuse to bully others now"
  • "Thank you Will Smith".
