Top Nollywood actor Tope Adebayo Salami has shared his side of the story amid allegations of defrauding an individual of over N2 million.

Taking to his official Instagram page, the actor categorically stated that he has never defrauded anyone, particularly the individual calling him out.

Adebayo explained that the man who called him out is a business partner and they both had plans of shooting a movie series back in 2019.
