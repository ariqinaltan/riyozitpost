Who plays Jamie in Top Boy?

Top Boy newcomer Jamie will be played by British actor Micheal Ward.

Living and working in London, Micheal is both an actor and a model and has even starred in campaigns for JD Sports and Foot Asylum.

Micheal will make his debut as Jamie in series 3 of Top Boy in Autumn 2019.

What is his character’s storyline?

In 2019 Micheal Ward will join the Top Boy cast as Jamie, the young, hungry and ruthless gang leader.

The new episodes of the series will begin as Dushane and Sully return to London to reclaim their place in the highly lucrative drug market.

However, with Jamie’s sights firmly set on taking over the drug trade, a rivalry begins between the three leaders.

Has he been on TV before?

Yes!

You might recognise Micheal from his roles in the Noel Clarke movie, Brotherhood, or the BBC series The A-List.

He’s also appeared in music videos for Stormzy and Lily Allen, and this year will star in a feature film adaptation of Rapman’s Youtube series Blue Story.
